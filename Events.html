The San Francisco De Sales School offers students great and fun experience not only academically but also physically. SFDSS hosts different kinds of events such as "The Acquaintance Party", "Intramurals", "Halloween Party" and more! Take a look of these events down below.

Events in our school

Sfdss tries to give the best experience to all students, to give them the one in a life time highschool life that they will remember.

Intramurals

Intramurals encourage school-wide involvement of all students in physical activity during non-instructional times that occur during the school day. Quality intramurals are student-focused, inclusive, fun events or activities that maximize physical activity participation for all students.

Acquaintance Party

These are annual getting-to-know-each-other parties of each branch for both old and new students and faculty alike, attended by the school administrators and management.

Halloween

A celebration observed in many countries on 31 October, the eve of the Western Christian feast of All Hallows' Day.
